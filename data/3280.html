57.3-8 IsFullRowModule
‣ IsFullRowModule( M )( property )

A full row module is a module R n superscript 𝑅 𝑛 R^{n} , for a ring R 𝑅 R and a nonnegative integer n 𝑛 n .

More precisely, a full row module is a free left module over a ring R 𝑅 R such that the elements are row vectors of the same length n 𝑛 n and with entries in R 𝑅 R and such that the dimension is equal to n 𝑛 n .

Several functions delegate their tasks to full row modules, for example Iterator (30.8-1) and Enumerator (30.3-2).
