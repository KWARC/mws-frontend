32.10-2 RespectsAdditiveInverses
‣ RespectsAdditiveInverses( mapp )( property )

Let mapp be a general mapping with underlying relation F ⊆ S × R 𝐹 𝑆 𝑅 F\subseteq S\times R , where S 𝑆 S and R 𝑅 R are the source and the range of mapp, respectively. Then RespectsAdditiveInverses returns true if S 𝑆 S and R 𝑅 R are additive-magmas-with-inverses such that ( s , r ) ∈ F 𝑠 𝑟 𝐹 (s,r)\in F implies ( - s , - r ) ∈ F 𝑠 𝑟 𝐹 (-s,-r)\in F , and false otherwise.

If mapp is single-valued then RespectsAdditiveInverses returns true if and only if the equation AdditiveInverse( s )^mapp = AdditiveInverse( s^mapp ) holds for all s in S 𝑆 S .
