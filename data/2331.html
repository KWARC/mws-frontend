55.2-3 NearAdditiveGroup
‣ NearAdditiveGroup( [Fam, ]gens )( function )

returns the (near-)additive group A 𝐴 A that is generated by the elements in the list gens, that is, the closure of gens under addition +, Zero (31.10-3), and AdditiveInverse (31.10-9). The family Fam of A 𝐴 A can be entered as first argument; this is obligatory if gens is empty (and hence A 𝐴 A is trivial).
