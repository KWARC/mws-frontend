57.1-1 IsLeftOperatorAdditiveGroup
‣ IsLeftOperatorAdditiveGroup( D )( category )

A domain D lies in IsLeftOperatorAdditiveGroup if it is an additive group that is closed under scalar multiplication from the left, and such that λ * ( x + y ) = λ * x + λ * y 𝜆 𝑥 𝑦 𝜆 𝑥 𝜆 𝑦 \lambda*(x+y)=\lambda*x+\lambda*y for all scalars λ 𝜆 \lambda and elements x , y ∈ D 𝑥 𝑦 𝐷 x,y\in D (here and below by scalars we mean elements of a domain acting on D from left or right as appropriate).
