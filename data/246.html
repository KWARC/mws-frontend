50.2-6 GeneralOrthogonalGroup
‣ GeneralOrthogonalGroup( [filt, ][e, ]d, q )( function )
‣ GO( [filt, ][e, ]d, q )( function )

constructs a group isomorphic to the general orthogonal group GO( e, d, q ) of those 𝑑 × 𝑑 d d \textit{d}\times\textit{d} matrices over the field with q elements that respect a non-singular quadratic form (see InvariantQuadraticForm (44.5-5)) specified by e, in the category given by the filter filt.

The value of e must be 0 0 for odd d (and can optionally be omitted in this case), respectively one of 1 1 1 or - 1 1 -1 for even d. If filt is not given it defaults to IsMatrixGroup (44.1-1), and the returned group is the general orthogonal group itself.

Note that in [KL90], GO is defined as the stabilizer Δ ⁢ ( V , F , κ ) Δ 𝑉 𝐹 𝜅 \Delta(V,F,\kappa) of the quadratic form, up to scalars, whereas our GO is called I ⁢ ( V , F , κ ) 𝐼 𝑉 𝐹 𝜅 I(V,F,\kappa) there.
