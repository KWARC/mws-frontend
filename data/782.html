66.17-14 MonomialExtGrlexLess
‣ MonomialExtGrlexLess( a, b )( function )

implements comparison of monomial in their external representation by a "grlex" order with x 1 ⁢ & ⁢ g ⁢ t ; x 2 subscript 𝑥 1 & 𝑔 𝑡 subscript 𝑥 2 x_{1}&gt;x_{2} (This is exactly the same as the ordering by MonomialGrlexOrdering (66.17-8), see  66.17). The function takes two monomials a and b in expanded form and returns whether the first is smaller than the second. (This ordering is also used by GAP internally for representing polynomials as a linear combination of monomials.)

See section 66.21 for details on the expanded form of monomials.
