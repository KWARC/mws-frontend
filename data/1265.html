47.14-7 LargerQuotientBySubgroupAbelianization
‣ LargerQuotientBySubgroupAbelianization( hom, U )( function )

Let hom a homomorphism from a finitely presented group G 𝐺 G to a finite group H 𝐻 H and 𝑈 ≤ H U 𝐻 \textit{U}\leq H . This function will -- if it exists -- return a subgroup S ≤ 𝐺 𝑆 G S\leq\textit{G} , such that the core of S 𝑆 S is properly contained in the kernel of hom as well as in V ′ superscript 𝑉 ′ V^{\prime} , where V 𝑉 V is the pre-image of U under hom. Thus S 𝑆 S exposes a larger quotient of G 𝐺 G . If no such subgroup exists, fail is returned.
