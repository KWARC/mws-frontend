50.2-14 ProjectiveSpecialUnitaryGroup
‣ ProjectiveSpecialUnitaryGroup( [filt, ]d, q )( function )
‣ PSU( [filt, ]d, q )( function )

constructs a group isomorphic to the projective special unitary group PSU( d, q ) of those 𝑑 × 𝑑 d d \textit{d}\times\textit{d} matrices over the field with 𝑞 2 superscript q 2 \textit{q}^{2} elements that respect a fixed nondegenerate sesquilinear form and have determinant 1, modulo the centre, in the category given by the filter filt.

If filt is not given it defaults to IsPermGroup (43.1-1), and the returned group is the action on lines of the underlying vector space.
