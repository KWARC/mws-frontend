39.24-9 BasicSpinRepresentationOfSymmetricGroup
‣ BasicSpinRepresentationOfSymmetricGroup( n, p, sign )( function )

Constructs the image of the Coxeter generators in the basic spin (projective) representation of the symmetric group of degree n over a field of characteristic 𝑝 ≥ 0 p 0 \textit{p}\geq 0 . There are two such representations and sign controls which is returned: +1 gives a group where the preimage of an adjacent transposition ( i , i + 1 ) 𝑖 𝑖 1 (i,i+1) has order 4, -1 gives a group where the preimage of an adjacent transposition ( i , i + 1 ) 𝑖 𝑖 1 (i,i+1) has order 2. If no sign is specified, +1 is used by default. If no p is specified, 3 is used by default. (Note that the convention of which cover is labelled as +1 is inconsistent in the literature.)
