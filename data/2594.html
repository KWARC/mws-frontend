61.10-3 LeftModuleHomomorphismByMatrix
‣ LeftModuleHomomorphismByMatrix( BS, matrix, BR )( operation )

Let BS and BR be bases of the left R 𝑅 R -modules V 𝑉 V and W 𝑊 W , respectively. LeftModuleHomomorphismByMatrix returns the R 𝑅 R -linear mapping from V 𝑉 V to W 𝑊 W that is defined by the matrix matrix, as follows. The image of the i 𝑖 i -th basis vector of BS is the linear combination of the basis vectors of BR with coefficients the i 𝑖 i -th row of matrix.

gap> V:= Rationals^2;;
gap> W:= VectorSpace( Rationals, [ [ 1, 0, 1 ], [ 1, 2, 3 ] ] );;
gap> f:= LeftModuleHomomorphismByMatrix( Basis( V ),
> [ [ 1, 2 ], [ 3, 1 ] ], Basis( W ) );
<linear mapping by matrix, ( Rationals^
2 ) -> <vector space over Rationals, with 2 generators>>
