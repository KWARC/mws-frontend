72.15-2 BrauerCharacterValue
‚Ä£ BrauerCharacterValue( mat )( attribute )

For an invertible matrix mat over a finite field F ùêπ F , BrauerCharacterValue returns the Brauer character value of mat if the order of mat is coprime to the characteristic of F ùêπ F , and fail otherwise.

The Brauer character value of a matrix is the sum of complex lifts of its eigenvalues.
