32.9-3 RespectsInverses
‣ RespectsInverses( mapp )( property )

Let mapp be a general mapping with underlying relation F ⊆ 𝑆 × 𝑅 𝐹 S R F\subseteq\textit{S}\times\textit{R} , where S and R are the source and the range of mapp, respectively. Then RespectsInverses returns true if S and R are magmas-with-inverses such that, for s ∈ 𝑆 𝑠 S s\in\textit{S} and r ∈ 𝑅 𝑟 R r\in\textit{R} , ( s , r ) ∈ F 𝑠 𝑟 𝐹 (s,r)\in F implies ( s - 1 , r - 1 ) ∈ F superscript 𝑠 1 superscript 𝑟 1 𝐹 (s^{{-1}},r^{{-1}})\in F , and false otherwise.

If mapp is single-valued then RespectsInverses returns true if and only if the equation Inverse( s )^mapp = Inverse( s^mapp ) holds for all s in S 𝑆 S .
