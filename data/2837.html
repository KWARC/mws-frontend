39.15-15 SmallSimpleGroup
‣ SmallSimpleGroup( order[, i] )( function )

Returns: The ith simple group of order order in the stored list, given in a small-degree permutation representation, or fail (20.2-1) if no such simple group exists.

If i is not given, it defaults to 1. Currently, all simple groups of order less than 10 6 superscript 10 6 10^{6} are available via this function.

gap> SmallSimpleGroup(60);
A5
gap> SmallSimpleGroup(20160,1);
A8
gap> SmallSimpleGroup(20160,2);
PSL(3,4)
