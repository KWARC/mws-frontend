45.9-6 CorrespondingGeneratorsByModuloPcgs
‣ CorrespondingGeneratorsByModuloPcgs( mpcgs, imgs )( function )

Let mpcgs be a modulo pcgs for a factor of a group G 𝐺 G and let U 𝑈 U be a subgroup of G 𝐺 G generated by imgs such that U 𝑈 U covers the factor for the modulo pcgs. Then this function computes elements in U 𝑈 U corresponding to the generators of the modulo pcgs.

Note that the computation of induced generating sets is not possible for some modulo pcgs.
