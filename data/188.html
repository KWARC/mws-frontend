If you want to perform calculations in such a matrix group G ùê∫ G you should be aware of the fact that the permutation group routines of GAP are much more efficient than the matrix group routines. Hence we recommend that you do your computations, whenever possible, in the isomorphic permutation group which is induced by the action of G ùê∫ G on one of the orbits of the associated short vectors. You may call one of the following functions IsomorphismPermGroup (50.12-5) or IsomorphismPermGroupImfGroup (50.12-6) to get an isomorphism to such a permutation group (note that these GAP¬†4 functions have replaced the GAP¬†3 functions PermGroup and PermGroupImfGroup).
