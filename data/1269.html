47.14-3 EpimorphismPGroup
‣ EpimorphismPGroup( fpgrp, p[, cl] )( operation )

computes an epimorphism from the finitely presented group fpgrp to the largest p 𝑝 p -group of p 𝑝 p -class cl which is a quotient of fpgrp. If cl is omitted, the largest finite p 𝑝 p -group quotient (of p 𝑝 p -class up to 1000 1000 1000 ) is determined.

gap> hom:=EpimorphismPGroup(fp,2);
[ f1, f2 ] -> [ a1, a2 ]
gap> Size(Image(hom));
8
gap> hom:=EpimorphismPGroup(fp,3,7);
[ f1, f2 ] -> [ a1, a2 ]
gap> Size(Image(hom));
6561
