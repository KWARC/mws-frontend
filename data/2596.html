61.10-1 LeftModuleGeneralMappingByImages
‣ LeftModuleGeneralMappingByImages( V, W, gens, imgs )( operation )

Let V and W be two left modules over the same left acting domain R 𝑅 R , say, and gens and imgs lists (of the same length) of elements in V and W, respectively. LeftModuleGeneralMappingByImages returns the general mapping with source V and range W that is defined by mapping the elements in gens to the corresponding elements in imgs, and taking the R 𝑅 R -linear closure.

gens need not generate V as a left R 𝑅 R -module, and if the specification does not define a linear mapping then the result will be multi-valued; hence in general it is not a mapping (see IsMapping (32.3-3)).

gap> V:= Rationals^2;;
gap> W:= VectorSpace( Rationals, [ [1,2,3], [1,0,1] ] );;
gap> f:= LeftModuleGeneralMappingByImages( V, W,
>                                [[1,0],[2,0]], [[1,0,1],[1,0,1] ] );
[ [ 1, 0 ], [ 2, 0 ] ] -> [ [ 1, 0, 1 ], [ 1, 0, 1 ] ]
gap> IsMapping( f );
false
