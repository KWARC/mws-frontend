55.1-5 IsAdditiveMagmaWithZero
‣ IsAdditiveMagmaWithZero( obj )( category )

An additive magma-with-zero in GAP is an additive magma A 𝐴 A (see IsAdditiveMagma (55.1-4) with an operation 0* (or Zero (31.10-3)) that yields the zero of A 𝐴 A .

So an additive magma-with-zero A 𝐴 A does always contain a unique additively neutral element z 𝑧 z , i.e., z + a = a = a + z 𝑧 𝑎 𝑎 𝑎 𝑧 z+a=a=a+z holds for all a ∈ A 𝑎 𝐴 a\in A (see AdditiveNeutralElement (55.3-5)). This element z 𝑧 z can be computed with the operation Zero (31.10-3) as Zero( A 𝐴 A ), and z 𝑧 z is also equal to Zero( a 𝑎 a ) and to 0* a 𝑎 a for each element a 𝑎 a in A 𝐴 A .

Note that it may happen that an additive magma containing a zero does not lie in the category IsAdditiveMagmaWithZero (see 31.6).
