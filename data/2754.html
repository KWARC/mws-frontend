39.24-1 EpimorphismSchurCover
‣ EpimorphismSchurCover( G[, pl] )( attribute )

returns an epimorphism e ⁢ p ⁢ i 𝑒 𝑝 𝑖 epi from a group D 𝐷 D onto G. The group D 𝐷 D is one (of possibly several) Schur covers of G. The group D 𝐷 D can be obtained as the Source (32.3-8) value of epi. The kernel of e ⁢ p ⁢ i 𝑒 𝑝 𝑖 epi is the Schur multiplier of G. If pl is given as a list of primes, only the multiplier part for these primes is realized. At the moment, D 𝐷 D is represented as a finitely presented group.
