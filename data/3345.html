64.11-2 NilpotentQuotientOfFpLieAlgebra

Here FpL is a finitely presented Lie algebra. Let K ùêæ K be the quotient of FpL by the max+1-th term of its lower central series. This function calculates a surjective homomorphism from FpL onto K ùêæ K . When called with the third argument weights, the k ùëò k -th generator of FpL gets assigned the k ùëò k -th element of the list weights. In that case a quotient is calculated of FpL by the ideal generated by all elements of weight max+1. If the list weights only consists of 1 1 1 's then the two calls are equivalent. The default value of weights is a list (of length equal to the number of generators of FpL) consisting of 1 1 1 's.

If the relators of FpL are homogeneous, then the resulting algebra is naturally graded.
