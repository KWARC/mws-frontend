TestInducedFromNormalSubgroup returns a record with information whether the irreducible character chi of the group G ùê∫ G , say, is induced from a proper normal subgroup of G ùê∫ G . If the second argument N is present, which must be a normal subgroup of G ùê∫ G or the list of class positions of a normal subgroup of G ùê∫ G , it is checked whether chi is induced from N.
