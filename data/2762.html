39.22-6 IndependentGeneratorExponents
‣ IndependentGeneratorExponents( G, g )( operation )

For an abelian group G, with IndependentGeneratorsOfAbelianGroup (39.22-5) value the list [ a 1 , … , a n ] subscript 𝑎 1 … subscript 𝑎 𝑛 [a_{1},\ldots,a_{n}] , this operation returns the exponent vector [ e 1 , … , e n ] subscript 𝑒 1 … subscript 𝑒 𝑛 [e_{1},\ldots,e_{n}] to represent 𝑔 = ∏ i a i e i g subscript product 𝑖 superscript subscript 𝑎 𝑖 subscript 𝑒 𝑖 \textit{g}=\prod_{i}a_{i}^{{e_{i}}} .

gap> g := AbelianGroup([16,9,625]);;
gap> gens := IndependentGeneratorsOfAbelianGroup(g);;
gap> List(gens, Order);
[ 9, 16, 625 ]
gap> AbelianInvariants(g);
[ 9, 16, 625 ]
gap> r:=gens[1]^4*gens[2]^12*gens[3]^128;;
