32.10-1 RespectsAddition
‣ RespectsAddition( mapp )( property )

Let mapp be a general mapping with underlying relation F ⊆ S × R 𝐹 𝑆 𝑅 F\subseteq S\times R , where S 𝑆 S and R 𝑅 R are the source and the range of mapp, respectively. Then RespectsAddition returns true if S 𝑆 S and R 𝑅 R are additive magmas such that ( s 1 , r 1 ) , ( s 2 , r 2 ) ∈ F subscript 𝑠 1 subscript 𝑟 1 subscript 𝑠 2 subscript 𝑟 2 𝐹 (s_{1},r_{1}),(s_{2},r_{2})\in F implies ( s 1 + s 2 , r 1 + r 2 ) ∈ F subscript 𝑠 1 subscript 𝑠 2 subscript 𝑟 1 subscript 𝑟 2 𝐹 (s_{1}+s_{2},r_{1}+r_{2})\in F , and false otherwise.
