73.5-10 CheckFixedPoints
‚Ä£ CheckFixedPoints( inside1, between, inside2 )( function )

Let inside1, between, inside2 be parametrized maps, where between is assumed to map each fixed point of inside1 (that is, inside1[ i ùëñ i ] = i) to a fixed point of inside2 (that is, between[ i ùëñ i ] is either an integer that is fixed by inside2 or a list that has nonempty intersection with the union of its images under inside2). CheckFixedPoints changes between and inside2 by removing all those entries violate this condition.

When an inconsistency is detected, CheckFixedPoints immediately returns fail. Otherwise the list of positions is returned where changes occurred.
