39.9-3 DoubleCosets
‣ DoubleCosets( G, U, V )( operation )
‣ DoubleCosetsNC( G, U, V )( operation )

computes a duplicate free list of all double cosets U g 𝑔 g V for g ∈ 𝐺 𝑔 G g\in\textit{G} . The groups U and V must be subgroups of the group G. The NC version does not check whether U and V are subgroups of G.

gap> dc:=DoubleCosets(g,u,v);
[ DoubleCoset(Group( [ (1,2,3), (1,2) ] ),(),Group( [ (3,4) ] )), 
  DoubleCoset(Group( [ (1,2,3), (1,2) ] ),(1,3)(2,4),Group( 
    [ (3,4) ] )), DoubleCoset(Group( [ (1,2,3), (1,2) ] ),(1,4)
    (2,3),Group( [ (3,4) ] )) ]
gap> List(dc,Representative);
[ (), (1,3)(2,4), (1,4)(2,3) ]
