16.2-30 PartitionTuples
‣ PartitionTuples( n, r )( function )

PartitionTuples returns the list of all r-tuples of partitions which together form a partition of n.

r-tuples of partitions describe the classes and the characters of wreath products of groups with r conjugacy classes with the symmetric group S n subscript 𝑆 𝑛 S_{n} .
