21.20-9 IsLexicographicallyLess
‣ IsLexicographicallyLess( list1, list2 )( function )

Let list1 and list2 be two dense, but not necessarily homogeneous lists (see IsDenseList (21.1-2), IsHomogeneousList (21.1-3)), such that for each i 𝑖 i , the entries in both lists at position i 𝑖 i can be compared via <. IsLexicographicallyLess returns true if list1 is smaller than list2 w.r.t. lexicographical ordering, and false otherwise.
