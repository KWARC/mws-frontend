Local presentability

Definition

An accessible monad is a monad on an accessible category whose underlying functor is an accessible functor.

Proposition

The Eilenberg-Moore category of a κ κ 𝜅 \kappa -accessible monad, def. 2, is a κ κ 𝜅 \kappa -accessible category. If in addition the category on which the monad acts is a κ κ 𝜅 \kappa -locally presentable category then so is the EM-category.

(Adamek-Rosicky, 2.78)
