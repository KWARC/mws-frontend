64.9-7 FindSl2
‣ FindSl2( L, x )( function )

This function tries to find a subalgebra S 𝑆 S of the Lie algebra L with S 𝑆 S isomorphic to s ⁢ l 2 𝑠 subscript 𝑙 2 sl_{2} and such that the nilpotent element x of L is contained in S 𝑆 S . If such an algebra exists then it is returned, otherwise fail is returned.

gap> L:= SimpleLieAlgebra( "G", 2, Rationals );;
gap> b:= BasisVectors( Basis( L ) );;
gap> IsNilpotentElement( L, b[1] );
true
gap> FindSl2( L, b[1] );
<Lie algebra of dimension 3 over Rationals>
