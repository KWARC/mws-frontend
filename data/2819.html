39.17-4 SubnormalSeries
‣ SubnormalSeries( G, U )( operation )

If U is a subgroup of G this operation returns a subnormal series that descends from G to a subnormal subgroup V ≥ 𝑉 absent V\geq U. If U is subnormal, V = 𝑉 absent V= U.

gap> s:=SubnormalSeries(g,Group((1,2)(3,4)));
[ Group([ (1,2,3,4), (1,2) ]), Group([ (1,2)(3,4), (1,3)(2,4) ]), 
  Group([ (1,2)(3,4) ]) ]
