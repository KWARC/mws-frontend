16.2-27 SignPartition
‣ SignPartition( pi )( function )

returns the sign of a permutation with cycle structure pi.

This function actually describes a homomorphism from the symmetric group S n subscript 𝑆 𝑛 S_{n} into the cyclic group of order 2, whose kernel is exactly the alternating group A n subscript 𝐴 𝑛 A_{n} (see SignPerm (42.4-1)). Partitions of sign 1 are called even partitions while partitions of sign - 1 1 -1 are called odd.

gap> SignPartition([6,5,4,3,2,1]);
-1
