70.9-11 FactorGroupTom
‣ FactorGroupTom( tom, n )( operation )

For a table of marks tom of the group G 𝐺 G , say, and the normal subgroup N 𝑁 N of G 𝐺 G corresponding to the n-th class of subgroups of tom, FactorGroupTom returns the table of marks of the factor group G / N 𝐺 𝑁 G/N .

gap> s4:= TableOfMarks( SymmetricGroup( 4 ) );
TableOfMarks( Sym( [ 1 .. 4 ] ) )
gap> LengthsTom( s4 );
[ 1, 3, 6, 4, 1, 3, 3, 4, 3, 1, 1 ]
gap> OrdersTom( s4 );
[ 1, 2, 2, 3, 4, 4, 4, 6, 8, 12, 24 ]
gap> s3:= FactorGroupTom( s4, 5 );
TableOfMarks( Group([ f1, f2 ]) )
gap> Display( s3 );
1:  6
2:  3 1
3:  2 . 2
4:  1 1 1 1
