72.9-3 InducedClassFunction
‣ InducedClassFunction( [tbl, ]chi, H )( operation )
‣ InducedClassFunction( [tbl, ]chi, hom )( operation )
‣ InducedClassFunction( [tbl, ]chi, suptbl )( operation )

Let chi be a class function of the group G 𝐺 G , say, and let target be either a supergroup H 𝐻 H of G 𝐺 G or an injective homomorphism from H 𝐻 H to G 𝐺 G or the character table of H. Then InducedClassFunction returns the class function of H 𝐻 H obtained by inducing chi to H 𝐻 H .
