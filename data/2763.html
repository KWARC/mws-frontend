39.22-5 IndependentGeneratorsOfAbelianGroup
‣ IndependentGeneratorsOfAbelianGroup( A )( attribute )

returns a list of generators a 1 , a 2 , … subscript 𝑎 1 subscript 𝑎 2 … a_{1},a_{2},\ldots of prime power order or infinite order of the abelian group A such that A is the direct product of the cyclic groups generated by the a i subscript 𝑎 𝑖 a_{i} . The list of orders of the returned generators must match the result of AbelianInvariants (39.16-1) (taking into account that zero and infinity (18.2-1) are identified).

gap> g:=AbelianGroup(IsPermGroup,[15,14,22,78]);;
gap> List(IndependentGeneratorsOfAbelianGroup(g),Order);
[ 2, 2, 2, 3, 3, 5, 7, 11, 13 ]
gap> AbelianInvariants(g);
[ 2, 2, 2, 3, 3, 5, 7, 11, 13 ]
