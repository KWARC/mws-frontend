61.7-3 IsNormalBasis
‣ IsNormalBasis( B )( property )

Let B be an S 𝑆 S -basis of a field F 𝐹 F , say, for a subfield S 𝑆 S of F 𝐹 F . IsNormalBasis returns true if B is invariant under the Galois group (see GaloisGroup (58.3-1)) of the field extension F / S 𝐹 𝑆 F/S , and false otherwise.
