43.4 Symmetric and Alternating Groups

The commands SymmetricGroup (50.1-10) and AlternatingGroup (50.1-9) (see SectionÂ 50.1) construct symmetric and alternating permutation groups. GAP can also detect whether a given permutation group is a symmetric or alternating group on the set of its moved points; if so then the group is called a natural symmetric or alternating group, respectively.

The functions IsSymmetricGroup (43.4-2) and IsAlternatingGroup (43.4-3) can be used to check whether a given group (not necessarily a permutation group) is isomorphic to a symmetric or alternating group.
