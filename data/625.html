72.8-15 IsTransitive
‣ IsTransitive( [tbl, ]chi )( property )

For a permutation character chi of the group G 𝐺 G that corresponds to an action on the G 𝐺 G -set Ω Ω \Omega (see PermutationCharacter (72.7-3)), IsTransitive (41.10-1) returns true if the action of G 𝐺 G on Ω Ω \Omega is transitive, and false otherwise.
