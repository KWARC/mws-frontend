31.13-6 InstallIsomorphismMaintenance
‣ InstallIsomorphismMaintenance( opr, old_req, new_req )( function )

opr must be a property or an attribute. The call of InstallIsomorphismMaintenance has the effect that for a domain D 𝐷 D in the filter old_req, and a domain E 𝐸 E in the filter new_req, the call UseIsomorphismRelation ( D , E ) 𝐷 𝐸 (D,E) (see UseIsomorphismRelation (31.13-3)) sets a known value of opr for D 𝐷 D as value of opr also for E 𝐸 E . A typical example for which InstallIsomorphismMaintenance is applied is given by opr = Size, old_req = IsCollection, and new_req = IsCollection.
