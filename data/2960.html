14.4-11 PrimePowersInt
‣ PrimePowersInt( n )( function )

returns the prime factorization of the integer n as a list [ p 1 , e 1 , … , p k , e k ] subscript 𝑝 1 subscript 𝑒 1 … subscript 𝑝 𝑘 subscript 𝑒 𝑘 [p_{1},e_{1},\ldots,p_{k},e_{k}] with n = p 1 e 1 ⋅ p 2 e 2 ⋅ … ⋅ p k e k ⋅ superscript subscript 𝑝 1 subscript 𝑒 1 superscript subscript 𝑝 2 subscript 𝑒 2 … superscript subscript 𝑝 𝑘 subscript 𝑒 𝑘 p_{1}^{{e_{1}}}\cdot p_{2}^{{e_{2}}}\cdot...\cdot p_{k}^{{e_{k}}} .
