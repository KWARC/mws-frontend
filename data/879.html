49.5 Free Products

Let G 𝐺 G and H 𝐻 H be groups with presentations ⟨ X ∣ R ⟩ inner-product 𝑋 𝑅 \langle X\mid R\rangle and ⟨ Y ∣ S ⟩ inner-product 𝑌 𝑆 \langle Y\mid S\rangle , respectively. Then the free product G * H 𝐺 𝐻 G*H is the group with presentation ⟨ X ∪ Y ∣ R ∪ S ⟩ inner-product 𝑋 𝑌 𝑅 𝑆 \langle X\cup Y\mid R\cup S\rangle . This construction can be generalized to an arbitrary number of groups.
