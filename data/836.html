66.6-6 Discriminant
â€£ Discriminant( pol[, ind] )( operation )

If pol is a univariate polynomial then Discriminant returns the discriminant of pol by its indeterminate. The two-argument form returns the discriminant of a polynomial pol by the indeterminate number ind, regarding pol as univariate in this indeterminate. Instead of the indeterminate number, the indeterminate itself can also be given as ind.
