72.8-16 Transitivity
‣ Transitivity( [tbl, ]chi )( attribute )

For a permutation character chi of the group G 𝐺 G that corresponds to an action on the G 𝐺 G -set Ω Ω \Omega (see PermutationCharacter (72.7-3)), Transitivity returns the maximal nonnegative integer k 𝑘 k such that the action of G 𝐺 G on Ω Ω \Omega is k 𝑘 k -transitive.

gap> IsTransitive( nat );  Transitivity( nat );
true
4
gap> Transitivity( 2 * TrivialCharacter( S4 ) );
0
