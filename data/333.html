59.2 Operations for Finite Field Elements

Since finite field elements are scalars, the operations Characteristic (31.10-1), One (31.10-2), Zero (31.10-3), Inverse (31.10-8), AdditiveInverse (31.10-9), Order (31.10-10) can be applied to them (seeÂ 31.10). Contrary to the situation with other scalars, Order (31.10-10) is defined also for the zero element in a finite field, with value 0.

gap> One( Z(9) );  One( 0*Z(4) );
