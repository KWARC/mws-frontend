40.7-6 InducedAutomorphism
‣ InducedAutomorphism( epi, aut )( function )

Let aut be an automorphism of a group G 𝐺 G and epi be a homomorphism from G 𝐺 G to a group H 𝐻 H such that the kernel of epi is fixed under aut. Let U 𝑈 U be the image of epi. This command returns the automorphism of U 𝑈 U induced by aut via epi, that is, the automorphism of U 𝑈 U which maps g 𝑔 g ^epi to ( g 𝑔 g ^aut)^epi, for g ∈ G 𝑔 𝐺 g\in G .

gap> g:=Group((1,2,3,4),(1,2));
Group([ (1,2,3,4), (1,2) ])
gap> n:=Subgroup(g,[(1,2)(3,4),(1,3)(2,4)]);
Group([ (1,2)(3,4), (1,3)(2,4) ])
gap> epi:=NaturalHomomorphismByNormalSubgroup(g,n);
[ (1,2,3,4), (1,2) ] -> [ f1*f2, f1 ]
gap> aut:=InnerAutomorphism(g,(1,2,3));
^(1,2,3)
gap> InducedAutomorphism(epi,aut);
^f2
