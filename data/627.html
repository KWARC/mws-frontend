72.8-13 InertiaSubgroup
‣ InertiaSubgroup( [tbl, ]G, chi )( operation )

Let chi be a character of the group H 𝐻 H , say, and tbl the character table of H 𝐻 H ; if the argument tbl is not given then the underlying character table of chi (see UnderlyingCharacterTable (72.2-1)) is used instead. Furthermore, let G be a group that contains H 𝐻 H as a normal subgroup.

InertiaSubgroup returns the stabilizer in G of chi, w.r.t. the action of G on the classes of H 𝐻 H via conjugation. In other words, InertiaSubgroup returns the group of all those elements g ∈ 𝐺 𝑔 G g\in\textit{G} that satisfy 𝑐ℎ𝑖 g = 𝑐ℎ𝑖 superscript chi 𝑔 chi \textit{chi}^{g}=\textit{chi} .

gap> der:= DerivedSubgroup( S4 );
Alt( [ 1 .. 4 ] )
gap> List( Irr( der ), chi -> InertiaSubgroup( S4, chi ) );
[ S4, Alt( [ 1 .. 4 ] ), Alt( [ 1 .. 4 ] ), S4 ]
