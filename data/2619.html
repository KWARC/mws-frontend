61.7-2 IsIntegralBasis
‣ IsIntegralBasis( B )( property )

Let B be an S 𝑆 S -basis of a field F 𝐹 F , say, for a subfield S 𝑆 S of F 𝐹 F , and let R 𝑅 R and M 𝑀 M be the rings of algebraic integers in S 𝑆 S and F 𝐹 F , respectively. IsIntegralBasis returns true if B is also an R 𝑅 R -basis of M 𝑀 M , and false otherwise.
