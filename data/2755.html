39.24 Schur Covers and Multipliers

Additional attributes and properties of a group can be derived from computing its Schur cover. For example, if G 𝐺 G is a finitely presented group, the derived subgroup of a Schur cover of G 𝐺 G is invariant and isomorphic to the NonabelianExteriorSquare (39.24-5) value of G 𝐺 G , see [BJR87].
