64.14-2 LatticeGeneratorsInUEA
‣ LatticeGeneratorsInUEA( L )( attribute )

Here L must be a semisimple Lie algebra of characteristic 0 0 . This function returns a list of generators of an admissible lattice in the universal enveloping algebra of L, relative to the Chevalley basis contained in ChevalleyBasis( L ) (see ChevalleyBasis (64.6-2)). First are listed the negative root vectors (denoted by y 1 , … , y s subscript 𝑦 1 … subscript 𝑦 𝑠 y_{1},\ldots,y_{s} ), then the positive root vectors (denoted by x 1 , … , x s subscript 𝑥 1 … subscript 𝑥 𝑠 x_{1},\ldots,x_{s} ). At the end of the list there are the Cartan elements. They are printed as ( hi/1 ), which means

\[ {{h_i \choose 1}}. \]

In general the printed form ( hi/ k ) means

\[ {{h_i \choose k}}. \]

Also y i ( m ) superscript subscript 𝑦 𝑖 𝑚 y_{i}^{(m)} is printed as yi^(m), which means that entering yi^m at the GAP prompt results in the output m!*yi^(m).

Products of lattice generators are collected using the following order: first come the y i ( m i ) superscript subscript 𝑦 𝑖 subscript 𝑚 𝑖 y_{i}^{(m_{i})} (in the same order as the positive roots), then the ( h i k i ) binomial subscript ℎ 𝑖 subscript 𝑘 𝑖 {h_{i}\choose k_{i}} , and then the x i ( n i ) superscript subscript 𝑥 𝑖 subscript 𝑛 𝑖 x_{i}^{(n_{i})} (in the same order as the positive roots).
