56.7-1 Gcd
‣ Gcd( [R, ]r1, r2, ... )( function )
‣ Gcd( [R, ]list )( function )

Gcd returns the greatest common divisor of the ring elements r1, r2, … … \ldots resp. of the ring elements in the list list in the ring R, if given, and otherwise in their default ring, see DefaultRing (56.1-3).

Gcd returns the standard associate (see StandardAssociate (56.5-5)) of the greatest common divisors.

A divisor of an element r 𝑟 r in the ring R 𝑅 R is an element d ∈ R 𝑑 𝑅 d\in R such that r 𝑟 r is a multiple of d 𝑑 d . A common divisor of the elements r 1 , r 2 , … subscript 𝑟 1 subscript 𝑟 2 … r_{1},r_{2},\ldots in the ring R 𝑅 R is an element d ∈ R 𝑑 𝑅 d\in R which is a divisor of each r 1 , r 2 , … subscript 𝑟 1 subscript 𝑟 2 … r_{1},r_{2},\ldots . A greatest common divisor d 𝑑 d in addition has the property that every other common divisor of r 1 , r 2 , … subscript 𝑟 1 subscript 𝑟 2 … r_{1},r_{2},\ldots is a divisor of d 𝑑 d .

Note that this in particular implies the following: For the zero element z 𝑧 z of R, we have Gcd( r, z 𝑧 z ) = Gcd( z 𝑧 z , r ) = StandardAssociate( r ) and Gcd( z 𝑧 z , z 𝑧 z ) = z 𝑧 z .
