39.12-3 DerivedSubgroup
‣ DerivedSubgroup( G )( attribute )

The derived subgroup 𝐺 ′ superscript G ′ \textit{G}^{\prime} of G is the subgroup generated by all commutators of pairs of elements of G. It is normal in G and the factor group 𝐺 / 𝐺 ′ G superscript G ′ \textit{G}/\textit{G}^{\prime} is the largest abelian factor group of G.

gap> g:=Group((1,2,3,4),(1,2));;
gap> DerivedSubgroup(g);
