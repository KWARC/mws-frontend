35.1-3 IsMagmaWithInversesIfNonzero
‣ IsMagmaWithInversesIfNonzero( obj )( category )

An object in this GAP category is a magma-with-one M 𝑀 M with an operation ^-1 : M ∖ Z → M ∖ Z : absent → 𝑀 𝑍 𝑀 𝑍 :M\setminus Z\rightarrow M\setminus Z that maps each element m 𝑚 m of M ∖ Z 𝑀 𝑍 M\setminus Z to its inverse m 𝑚 m ^-1 (or Inverse( m 𝑚 m ), see Inverse (31.10-8)), where Z 𝑍 Z is either empty or consists exactly of one element of M 𝑀 M .

This category was introduced mainly to describe division rings, since the nonzero elements in a division ring form a group; So an object M 𝑀 M in IsMagmaWithInversesIfNonzero will usually have both a multiplicative and an additive structure (see 55), and the set Z 𝑍 Z , if it is nonempty, contains exactly the zero element (see Zero (31.10-3)) of M 𝑀 M .
