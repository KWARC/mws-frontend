35.2-3 MagmaWithInverses
‣ MagmaWithInverses( [Fam, ]gens )( function )

returns the magma-with-inverses M 𝑀 M that is generated by the elements in the list gens, that is, the closure of gens under multiplication \* (31.12-1), One (31.10-2), and Inverse (31.10-8). The family Fam of M 𝑀 M can be entered as first argument; this is obligatory if gens is empty (and hence M 𝑀 M is trivial).
