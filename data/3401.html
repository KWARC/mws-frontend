64.3-2 LieCentralizer
‣ LieCentralizer( L, S )( operation )

is the annihilator of S in the Lie algebra L, that is, the set { a ∈ L : ∀ s ∈ S : a * s = 0 } conditional-set 𝑎 𝐿 : for-all 𝑠 𝑆 𝑎 𝑠 0 \{a\in L:\forall s\in S:a*s=0\} . Here S may be a subspace or a subalgebra of L.

gap> L:= SimpleLieAlgebra( "G", 2, Rationals );
<Lie algebra of dimension 14 over Rationals>
gap> b:= BasisVectors( Basis( L ) );;
gap> LieCentralizer( L, Subalgebra( L, [ b[1], b[2] ] ) );
<Lie algebra of dimension 1 over Rationals>
