52.2-1 \/
‣ \/( F, rels )( method )

creates a finitely presented semigroup given by the presentation ⟨ g ⁢ e ⁢ n ⁢ s ∣ 𝑟𝑒𝑙𝑠 ⟩ inner-product 𝑔 𝑒 𝑛 𝑠 rels \langle gens\mid\textit{rels}\rangle where g ⁢ e ⁢ n ⁢ s 𝑔 𝑒 𝑛 𝑠 gens are the generators of the free semigroup F, and the relations rels are entered as pairs of words in the generators of the free semigroup.

The same result is obtained with the infix operator /, i.e., as F / rels.

gap> f:=FreeSemigroup(3);;
gap> s:=GeneratorsOfSemigroup(f);;
gap> f/[ [s[1]*s[2]*s[1],s[1]] , [s[2]^4,s[1]] ];
