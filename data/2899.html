39.9-2 RepresentativesContainedRightCosets
‣ RepresentativesContainedRightCosets( D )( attribute )

A double coset 𝐷 = U ⁢ g ⁢ V D 𝑈 𝑔 𝑉 \textit{D}=UgV can be considered as a union of right cosets U ⁢ h i 𝑈 subscript ℎ 𝑖 Uh_{i} . (It is the union of the orbit of U ⁢ g 𝑈 𝑔 Ug under right multiplication by V 𝑉 V .) For a double coset D this function returns a set of representatives h i subscript ℎ 𝑖 h_{i} such that D = ⋃ h i U ⁢ h i absent subscript subscript ℎ 𝑖 𝑈 subscript ℎ 𝑖 =\bigcup_{{h_{i}}}Uh_{i} . The representatives returned are canonical for U 𝑈 U (see CanonicalRightCosetElement (39.7-3)) and form a set.

gap> u:=Subgroup(g,[(1,2,3),(1,2)]);;v:=Subgroup(g,[(3,4)]);;
gap> c:=DoubleCoset(u,(2,4),v);
DoubleCoset(Group( [ (1,2,3), (1,2) ] ),(2,4),Group( [ (3,4) ] ))
gap> (1,2,3) in c;
false
gap> (2,3,4) in c;
true
gap> LeftActingGroup(c);
Group([ (1,2,3), (1,2) ])
gap> RightActingGroup(c);
Group([ (3,4) ])
gap> RepresentativesContainedRightCosets(c);
[ (2,3,4) ]
