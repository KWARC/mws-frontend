21.14-4 Division of lists

For two GAP objects x 𝑥 x and y 𝑦 y of which one is in IsMultiplicativeGeneralizedRowVector (21.12-2) and the other is also in IsMultiplicativeGeneralizedRowVector (21.12-2) or is neither a list nor a domain, x / y 𝑥 𝑦 x/y is defined as x * y - 1 𝑥 superscript 𝑦 1 x*y^{{-1}} .

gap> [ 1, 2, 3 ] / 2;  [ 1, 2 ] / [ [ 1, 2 ], [ 3, 4 ] ];
[ 1/2, 1, 3/2 ]
[ 1, 0 ]
