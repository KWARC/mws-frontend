58.1-3 Field
‣ Field( z, ... )( function )
‣ Field( [F, ]list )( function )

Field returns the smallest field K 𝐾 K that contains all the elements 𝑧 , … z … \textit{z},\ldots , or the smallest field K 𝐾 K that contains all elements in the list list. If no subfield F is given, K 𝐾 K is constructed as a field over itself, i.e. the left acting domain of K 𝐾 K is K 𝐾 K . Called with a field F and a list list, Field constructs the field generated by F and the elements in list, as a vector space over F.
