43.8-4 StabChainBaseStrongGenerators
‣ StabChainBaseStrongGenerators( base, sgs, one )( function )

Let base be a base for a permutation group G 𝐺 G , and let sgs be a strong generating set for G 𝐺 G with respect to base; one must be the appropriate identity element of G 𝐺 G (see One (31.10-2), in most cases this will be ()). This function constructs a stabilizer chain without the need to find Schreier generators; so this is much faster than the other algorithms.
