39.15-16 AllSmallNonabelianSimpleGroups
â€£ AllSmallNonabelianSimpleGroups( orders )( function )

Returns: A list of all nonabelian simple groups whose order lies in the range orders.

The groups are given in small-degree permutation representations. The returned list is sorted by ascending group order. Currently, all simple groups of order less than 10 6 superscript 10 6 10^{6} are available via this function.
