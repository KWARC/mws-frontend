39.9-1 DoubleCoset
‣ DoubleCoset( U, g, V )( operation )

The groups U and V must be subgroups of a common supergroup G of which g is an element. This command constructs the double coset U g V which is the set of all elements of the form u ⁢ g ⁢ v 𝑢 𝑔 𝑣 ugv for any u ∈ 𝑈 𝑢 U u\in\textit{U} , v ∈ 𝑉 𝑣 V v\in\textit{V} . For element operations such as in, a double coset behaves like a set of group elements. The double coset stores U in the attribute LeftActingGroup, g as Representative (30.4-7), and V as RightActingGroup.
