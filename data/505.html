71.12-4 IsClassFusionOfNormalSubgroup
‣ IsClassFusionOfNormalSubgroup( subtbl, fus, tbl )( function )

For two ordinary character tables tbl and subtbl of a group G 𝐺 G and its subgroup U 𝑈 U , say, and a list fus of positive integers that describes the class fusion of U 𝑈 U into G 𝐺 G , IsClassFusionOfNormalSubgroup returns true if U 𝑈 U is a normal subgroup of G 𝐺 G , and false otherwise.

gap> tblc2:= CharacterTable( "Cyclic", 2 );;
gap> tbld8:= CharacterTable( "Dihedral", 8 );;
gap> fus:= PossibleClassFusions( tblc2, tbld8 );
[ [ 1, 3 ], [ 1, 4 ], [ 1, 5 ] ]
gap> List(fus, map -> IsClassFusionOfNormalSubgroup(tblc2, map, tbld8));
[ true, false, false ]
