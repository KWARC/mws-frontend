41.10-1 IsTransitive
‣ IsTransitive( G, Omega[, gens, acts][, act] )( operation )
‣ IsTransitive( G )( property )
‣ IsTransitive( xset )( property )

returns true if the action implied by the arguments is transitive, or false otherwise.

We say that a group G acts transitively on a domain D 𝐷 D if and only if for every pair of points d , e ∈ D 𝑑 𝑒 𝐷 d,e\in D there is an element g 𝑔 g in G such that d g = e superscript 𝑑 𝑔 𝑒 d^{g}=e .

For permutation groups, the syntax IsTransitive(G) is also permitted and tests whether the group is transitive on the points moved by it, that is the group ⟨ ( 2 , 3 , 4 ) , ( 2 , 3 ) ⟩ 2 3 4 2 3 \langle(2,3,4),(2,3)\rangle is transitive (on 3 points).
