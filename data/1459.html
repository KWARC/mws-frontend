24.7-8 BaseFixedSpace
‣ BaseFixedSpace( mats )( function )

BaseFixedSpace returns a list of row vectors that form a base of the vector space V 𝑉 V such that v ⁢ M = v 𝑣 𝑀 𝑣 vM=v for all v 𝑣 v in V 𝑉 V and all matrices M 𝑀 M in the list mats. (This is the common eigenspace of all matrices in mats for the eigenvalue 1.)

gap> BaseFixedSpace([[[1,2],[0,1]]]);
[ [ 0, 1 ] ]
