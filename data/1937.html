43.10-12 SiftedPermutation
‣ SiftedPermutation( S, g )( function )

sifts the permutation g through the stabilizer chain S and returns the result after the last step.

The element g is sifted as follows: g is replaced by g * InverseRepresentative( S, S.orbit[1]^g ), then S is replaced by S.stabilizer and this process is repeated until S is trivial or S.orbit[1]^g is not in the basic orbit S.orbit. The remainder g is returned, it is the identity permutation if and only if the original g is in the group G 𝐺 G described by the original S.
