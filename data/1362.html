35.2-1 Magma
‣ Magma( [Fam, ]gens )( function )

returns the magma M 𝑀 M that is generated by the elements in the list gens, that is, the closure of gens under multiplication \* (31.12-1). The family Fam of M 𝑀 M can be entered as the first argument; this is obligatory if gens is empty (and hence also M 𝑀 M is empty).
