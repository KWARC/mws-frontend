22.4-4 SubtractBlist
‣ SubtractBlist( blist1, blist2 )( function )

subtracts the boolean list blist2 from the boolean list blist1, which must have equal length. This is equivalent to assigning blist1 [ i ] := assign delimited-[] 𝑖 absent [i]:= blist1 [ i ] delimited-[] 𝑖 [i] and not blist2 [ i ] delimited-[] 𝑖 [i] for all i 𝑖 i .

The function DifferenceBlist (22.3-3) is the nondestructive counterpart to SubtractBlist.
