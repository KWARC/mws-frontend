39.3-6 IsNormal
‣ IsNormal( G, U )( operation )

returns true if the group G normalizes the group U and false otherwise.

A group G normalizes a group U if and only if for every g ∈ 𝐺 𝑔 G g\in\textit{G} and u ∈ 𝑈 𝑢 U u\in\textit{U} the element u g superscript 𝑢 𝑔 u^{g} is a member of U. Note that U need not be a subgroup of G.

gap> IsNormal(g,u);
false
