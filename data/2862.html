39.13 Sylow Subgroups and Hall Subgroups

With respect to the following GAP functions, please note that by theorems of P. Hall, a group G 𝐺 G is solvable if and only if one of the following conditions holds.

  1. For each prime p 𝑝 p dividing the order of G 𝐺 G , there exists a p 𝑝 p -complement (see SylowComplement (39.13-2)).

  2. For each set P 𝑃 P of primes dividing the order of G 𝐺 G , there exists a P 𝑃 P -Hall subgroup (see HallSubgroup (39.13-3)).

  3. G 𝐺 G has a Sylow system (see SylowSystem (39.13-4)).

  4. G 𝐺 G has a complement system (see ComplementSystem (39.13-5)).
