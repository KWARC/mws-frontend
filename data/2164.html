69.2-1 PermutationGModule
‣ PermutationGModule( G, F )( function )

Called with a permutation group G and a finite field F, PermutationGModule returns the natural permutation module M 𝑀 M over F for the group of permutation matrices that acts on the canonical basis of M 𝑀 M in the same way as G acts on the points up to its largest moved point (see LargestMovedPoint (42.3-2)).
