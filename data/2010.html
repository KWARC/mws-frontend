44.3-3 BlowUpIsomorphism
‣ BlowUpIsomorphism( matgrp, B )( function )

For a matrix group matgrp and a basis B of a field extension L / K 𝐿 𝐾 L/K , say, such that the entries of all matrices in matgrp lie in L 𝐿 L , BlowUpIsomorphism returns the isomorphism with source matgrp that is defined by mapping the matrix A 𝐴 A to BlownUpMat ( A , 𝐵 ) 𝐴 B (A,\textit{B}) , see BlownUpMat (24.13-3).

gap> g:= GL(2,4);;
gap> B:= CanonicalBasis( GF(4) );;  BasisVectors( B );
[ Z(2)^0, Z(2^2) ]
gap> iso:= BlowUpIsomorphism( g, B );;
gap> Display( Image( iso, [ [ Z(4), Z(2) ], [ 0*Z(2), Z(4)^2 ] ] ) );
 . 1 1 .
 1 1 . 1
 . . 1 1
 . . 1 .
gap> img:= Image( iso, g );
<matrix group with 2 generators>
gap> Index( GL(4,2), img );
112
