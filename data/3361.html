This function computes the image of an element x of a restricted Lie algebra under its p ùëù p -map.

In the first form, a basis of the Lie algebra is provided; this basis stores the p ùëù p th powers of its elements. It is the traditional form, provided for backwards compatibility.

In its second form, only the element x is provided. It is the only form for elements of Lie algebras with no predetermined basis, such as those constructed by LieObject (64.1-1).

In its third form, an extra non-negative integer n is specified; the p ùëù p -mapping is iterated n times on the element x.
