21.14-6 Left quotients of lists

For two GAP objects x 𝑥 x and y 𝑦 y of which one is in IsMultiplicativeGeneralizedRowVector (21.12-2) and the other is also in IsMultiplicativeGeneralizedRowVector (21.12-2) or is neither a list nor a domain, LeftQuotient( x, y ) is defined as x - 1 * y superscript 𝑥 1 𝑦 x^{{-1}}*y .

gap> LeftQuotient( [ [ 1, 2 ], [ 3, 4 ] ], [ 1, 2 ] );
[ 0, 1/2 ]
