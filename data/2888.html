39.10-6 RationalClass
‣ RationalClass( G, g )( operation )

creates the rational class in G with representative g. A rational class consists of all elements that are conjugate to g or to an i 𝑖 i -th power of g where i 𝑖 i is coprime to the order of g 𝑔 g . Thus a rational class can be interpreted as a conjugacy class of cyclic subgroups. A rational class is an external set (IsExternalSet (41.12-1)) of group elements with the group acting by conjugation on it, but not an external orbit.
