24.14-4 SimultaneousEigenvalues
‣ SimultaneousEigenvalues( matlist, expo )( function )

The matrices in matlist must be matrices over GF(q) for some prime q. Together, they must generate an abelian p-group of exponent expo. Then the eigenvalues of mat in the splitting field GF(q^r) for some r are powers of an element ξ 𝜉 \xi in the splitting field, which is of order expo. SimultaneousEigenvalues returns a matrix of integers mod expo, say ( a i , j ) subscript 𝑎 𝑖 𝑗 (a_{{i,j}}) , such that the power ξ a i , j superscript 𝜉 subscript 𝑎 𝑖 𝑗 \xi^{{a_{{i,j}}}} is an eigenvalue of the i-th matrix in matlist and the eigenspaces of the different matrices to the eigenvalues ξ a i , j superscript 𝜉 subscript 𝑎 𝑖 𝑗 \xi^{{a_{{i,j}}}} for fixed j are equal.
