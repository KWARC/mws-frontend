A general comment:

The polynomials with which PadicExtensionNumberFamily is called define an extension of Q p subscript 𝑄 𝑝 Q_{p} . It must be ensured that both polynomials are really irreducible over Q p subscript 𝑄 𝑝 Q_{p} ! For example x 2 + x + 1 superscript 𝑥 2 𝑥 1 x^{2}+x+1 is not irreducible over Q p subscript 𝑄 𝑝 Q_{p} . Therefore the "extension" PadicExtensionNumberFamily(3, 4, [1,1,1], [1,1]) contains non-invertible "pseudo-p-adic numbers". Conversely, if an "extension" contains noninvertible elements then one of the defining polynomials was not irreducible.
