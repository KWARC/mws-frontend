21.14-1 One for lists

The one (seeÂ One (31.10-2)) of a dense list x in IsMultiplicativeGeneralizedRowVector (21.12-2) such that x has even multiplicative nesting depth and has the same length as each of its rows is defined as the usual identity matrix on the outer two levels, that is, an identity matrix of the same dimensions, with diagonal entries One( x[1][1] ) and off-diagonal entries Zero( x[1][1] ).
