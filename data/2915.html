39.5 Expressing Group Elements as Words in Generators

Using homomorphisms (see chapter 40) is is possible to express group elements as words in given generators: Create a free group (see FreeGroup (37.2-1)) on the correct number of generators and create a homomorphism from this free group onto the group G in whose generators you want to factorize. Then the preimage of an element of G is a word in the free generators, that will map on this element again.
