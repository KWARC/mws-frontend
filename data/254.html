50.1-12 SuzukiGroup
‣ SuzukiGroup( [filt, ]q )( function )
‣ Sz( [filt, ]q )( function )

Constructs a group isomorphic to the Suzuki group Sz( q ) over the field with q elements, where q is a non-square power of 2 2 2 .

If filt is not given it defaults to IsMatrixGroup (44.1-1), and the returned group is the Suzuki group itself.

gap> SuzukiGroup( 32 );
Sz(32)
