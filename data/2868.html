39.12-7 PrefrattiniSubgroup
‣ PrefrattiniSubgroup( G )( attribute )

returns a Prefrattini subgroup of the finite solvable group G.

A factor M / N 𝑀 𝑁 M/N of G is called a Frattini factor if M / N 𝑀 𝑁 M/N is contained in the Frattini subgroup of 𝐺 / N G 𝑁 \textit{G}/N . A subgroup P 𝑃 P is a Prefrattini subgroup of G if P 𝑃 P covers each Frattini chief factor of G, and if for each maximal subgroup of G there exists a conjugate maximal subgroup, which contains P 𝑃 P . In a finite solvable group G the Prefrattini subgroups form a characteristic conjugacy class of subgroups and the intersection of all these subgroups is the Frattini subgroup of G.
