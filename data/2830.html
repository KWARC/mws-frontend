39.15-22 RankPGroup
‣ RankPGroup( G )( attribute )

For a p 𝑝 p -group G (see IsPGroup (39.15-19)), RankPGroup returns the rank of G, which is defined as the minimal size of a generating system of G. If G is not a p 𝑝 p -group then an error is issued.

gap> h:=Group((1,2,3,4),(1,3));;
gap> PClassPGroup(h);
2
gap> RankPGroup(h);
2
