61.6-3 Coefficients
‣ Coefficients( B, v )( operation )

Let V 𝑉 V be the underlying left module of the basis B, and v a vector such that the family of v is the elements family of the family of V 𝑉 V . Then Coefficients( B, v ) is the list of coefficients of v w.r.t. B if v lies in V 𝑉 V , and fail otherwise.

gap> V:= VectorSpace( Rationals, [ [ 1, 2, 7 ], [ 1/2, 1/3, 5 ] ] );;
gap> B:= Basis( V, [ [ 1, 2, 7 ], [ 0, 1, -9/4 ] ] );;
gap> Coefficients( B, [ 1/2, 1/3, 5 ] );
[ 1/2, -2/3 ]
gap> Coefficients( B, [ 1, 0, 0 ] );
fail
