18.1-9 RoundCyc
‣ RoundCyc( cyc )( operation )

is a cyclotomic integer z 𝑧 z (see IsIntegralCyclotomic (18.1-4)) near to the cyclotomic cyc in the following sense: Let c be the i 𝑖 i -th coefficient in the external representation (see CoeffsCyc (18.1-10)) of cyc. Then the i 𝑖 i -th coefficient in the external representation of z 𝑧 z is Int( c + 1/2 ) or Int( c - 1/2 ), depending on whether c is nonnegative or negative, respectively.

Expressed in terms of the Zumbroich basis (see 60.3), rounding the coefficients of cyc w.r.t. this basis to the nearest integer yields the coefficients of z 𝑧 z .
