43.12-3 TwoClosure
‣ TwoClosure( G )( attribute )

The 2-closure of a transitive permutation group G on n 𝑛 n points is the largest subgroup of the symmetric group S n subscript 𝑆 𝑛 S_{n} which has the same orbits on sets of ordered pairs of points as the group G has. It also can be interpreted as the stabilizer of the orbital graphs of G.

gap> TwoClosure(Group((1,2,3),(2,3,4)));
Sym( [ 1 .. 4 ] )
