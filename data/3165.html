41.10-2 Transitivity
‣ Transitivity( G, Omega[, gens, acts][, act] )( operation )
‣ Transitivity( xset )( attribute )

returns the degree k 𝑘 k (a non-negative integer) of transitivity of the action implied by the arguments, i.e. the largest integer k 𝑘 k such that the action is k 𝑘 k -transitive. If the action is not transitive 0 is returned.

An action is k 𝑘 k -transitive if every k 𝑘 k -tuple of points can be mapped simultaneously to every other k 𝑘 k -tuple.

gap> Transitivity(g,[1..4]);
